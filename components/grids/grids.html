Grids

Grids provide the a way in which you can structure the content into rows and columns. They make your site look good and responsive as well.

Grids can be of different types based on requirement. Here there are 2 types - 2 Grid layout and 3 Grid layout

Below examples illustrate the 2 different types of grids. To use any of these grids, simply add class grid__container followed by its type.

Example → class="grid__container grid-2"

Rating

Rating are used to show how good the product is or giving points to something.

Rating are usually seen in E-Commerce websites, Movie Reviews

The below examples illustrate the different types of ratings. To use any of these ratings use proper class name and its type.

Rating - Filled

Example → class="rating__fill"
For Hover effect → class="rating__fill__hover"

Rating - Outline

Example → class="rating__outline__hover"
